What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
GEO is the practice of optimizing your content so AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude recommend your brand when users ask relevant questions. Unlike traditional SEO which targets Google rankings, GEO targets AI-generated answers. As AI search traffic grows 500%+ year-over-year, GEO is becoming essential for any online business.
How does Visora actually track AI visibility?
Visora sends 50 carefully crafted questions — the kind your target customers actually ask AI assistants — to AI platforms like Perplexity. We analyze each response to detect brand mentions, citation sources, and contextual framing. The data is real: it reflects what AI systems are recommending right now, not a simulation.
Why does Perplexity matter more than ChatGPT for tracking?
Perplexity is a real-time search engine — it crawls the web and shows its sources. This makes it the most transparent platform to track: you can see exactly which websites and articles lead to a brand being cited. ChatGPT uses training data that updates periodically, making it harder to attribute changes to specific actions. Perplexity is our primary data source because it gives actionable signals.
What's the difference between Visora and SEO tools like Ahrefs?
Traditional SEO tools track Google rankings and backlinks. Visora tracks AI citations — a completely different distribution channel. A page can rank #1 on Google and still be invisible to ChatGPT. Visora bridges this gap: it tells you not just where you rank, but whether AI systems are recommending you, and what specifically needs to change for them to start.
