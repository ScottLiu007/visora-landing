Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Visora ("we", "our", or "us") operates visoraapp.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or create an account
  • Website URL — when you use our GEO scanning service
  • Payment information — processed securely by Creem.io (we do not store card details)

We also collect usage data automatically: pages visited, features used, and scan results associated with your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Visora service
  • To send you scan reports and product updates (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Creem.io — payment processing (Merchant of Record)
  • Perplexity API — to perform AI visibility scans on your behalf
  • Cloudflare — hosting and security

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@visoraapp.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@visoraapp.com.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and regular security reviews.
