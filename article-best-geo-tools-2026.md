## What to Look for in a GEO Tool

Before comparing tools, understand what actually matters:

**Tracking accuracy**: Does the tool send real queries to real AI platforms, or simulate results? Real queries produce actionable data.

**Citation analysis**: Can it tell you *why* competitors appear in AI results? Knowing the citation sources is the key to fixing the gap.

**Execution support**: Does the tool just show you problems, or does it help you fix them? Most tools stop at monitoring.

**Pricing**: Enterprise tools start at $250-6,000/month. For indie hackers, this is prohibitive. Look for tools under $50/month.
